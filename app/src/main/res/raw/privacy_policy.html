WalkieTalkie Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how WalkieTalkie ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or other identifying information through our App.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Our App does not collect any usage data, analytics, or device information automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use any information for any purposes.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any information because we do not collect any information.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect the confidentiality of any information that might be collected, although we do not currently collect any personal data.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, no international data transfers occur.

7. Your Choices

You can choose not to use our App if you do not agree with this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@walkietalkie.app

10. Audio Communication Specifics

Our App enables voice communication between devices on the same local network. All audio communication:

  • Occurs directly between devices
  • Is not recorded or stored by our servers
  • Is not transmitted over the internet
  • Remains entirely on your local network

11. Permissions

Our App requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone Permission: Required to record and transmit your voice
  • Internet Permission: Required for local network communication
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep the communication service running

These permissions are only used for the core functionality of the App and are not used to collect any personal data.

12. Third-Party Services

Our App does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

13. Data Retention

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data retention period.

14. Your Rights

Under certain laws, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since we do not collect any personal data, these rights do not apply to our App.

15. California Privacy Rights

California residents have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect any personal information, these rights do not apply to our App.

16. European Union Users

If you are located in the European Union, you should know that we do not collect or process any personal data that would be subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

17. Consent

By using our App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
